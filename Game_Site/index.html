P5 js Boardgames

By Brian Kang

Omok

2 Players take turns to connect 5 dots in a row in any direction on the board.

Halli Galli

2 Players take turns flipping cards. Each card has a between 1 and 5 fruits of four different colors on them. If the the one there are a total of 5 fruits of the same color (no more, no less) displayed at once after flipping, the first player to hit the bell scores a point (left player hits the 'a' key and right player hits the 'l' key.)
